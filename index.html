The insanelyi repository

extensive, unique and free. After jailbreaking your device, follow the instructions directly below.
Cydia Repository
Open Cydia > Manage > Sources > Edit > Add > http://repo.insanelyi.com

Packages

The insanelyi repository offers a wide selection of tweaks, themes, apps, custom sounds, modifications, utilities and much more for your iPhone, iPod, iPad and AppleTV. Currently hosting over 9900 Packages, there is something for everyone.

Community

Come visit our forum, a community full of Apple iDevice lovers just like you. If your ever stuck and need a hand, we're here to help. Some features include: Blogs, Downloads, Tutorials, Gallery, Arcade and more.

Updates

A handy feature, browse to any Package on the repository, you can visit the “Recent” page, this page has a list of 60 recent new/updates Packages. Follow @insanelyi for daily notifications.

AppleTV

To add the repository your going to need a terminal prompt or Putty (Windows), ssh into your AppleTV with ssh root@apple-tv.local now execute echo "deb http://repo.insanelyi.com ios free" > /etc/apt/sources.list.d/insanelyi.list and to update the package list execute apt-get update
