How it works: No matter where you are on the forum, pull to refresh will reload the current url you are on, as for the views that are expected to navigate away from the forum, home, new content etc, in the past if you go to a site that is not exactly mobile friendly and unable to use the back gesture, pull to refresh will navigate back to the forum (will not work if you are on store and a couple other views, as they are not meant to navigate). Ex. If you're on the home tab, go to said site, and refresh, it will take you back to the home view. So you can nav away on w/e site when you want to return, just pull.
